Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync data between devices?

Sign in with the same Apple ID or email account on all devices. Your data will sync automatically.

How do I share access with my partner?

Go to Settings → Family Sharing → Invite Caregiver. They will receive an invite code to join.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings on your iPhone → Your Name → Subscriptions → Nestling → Cancel.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data.

Is my baby's data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
